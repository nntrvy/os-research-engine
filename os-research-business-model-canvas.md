# OS Research Business Model Canvas

**Company:** OS Research — Startup Building & Validation Studio (Vietnam)

---

## Customer Segments
- **Greedy Venture Capitalists** — Want maximum returns, minimum risk
- **Rich Angel Investors** — Have money, lack time/expertise to vet deals
- **Aspiring Startup Founders** — Have ideas but need validation confidence

---

## Value Propositions
- **De-risked startup deals with evidence** (for investors)
- **Data-backed due diligence done for you** (for investors)
- **Validation before you quit your job** (for founders)

---

## Channels
- **Exclusive deal flow drops**

---

## Customer Relationships
- **Co-investors, not consultants**

## Revenue Streams
- Return on investment in successful startups
