## Advertiser Pain Points

### From Social Listening Analysis

| Pain Point | Severity | Quote |
|------------|----------|-------|
| **Cannot Measure ROI** | High | "OOH highly impactful but notoriously difficult to measure" |
| **No Targeting** | High | Billboards reach everyone indiscriminately |
| **High Costs** | High | Billboard rental: tens to hundreds of millions VND |
| **Long Commitments** | Medium | Clients reluctant to commit long-term |
| **No Standardization** | Medium | Different providers use different methodologies |
| **Limited Engagement** | Medium | Only 5-7 seconds for billboard viewing |

### Jobs-to-be-Done (Advertisers)

**Functional:**
- Reach specific target audiences with outdoor advertising
- Measure advertising effectiveness and prove ROI
- Launch campaigns quickly without long-term commitments
- Target ads based on location, demographics, behavior

**Emotional:**
- Feel confident their advertising investment is working
- Avoid anxiety about wasted ad spend on wrong audiences

### Desired Gains

**Must-Haves:**
- Accurate impression counts and reach metrics
- Proof of campaign effectiveness with concrete data
- Ability to target specific demographics and locations

**Nice-to-Haves:**
- Real-time campaign optimization
- Dynamic creative (adapts to time, weather, events)
- Lower CPMs than traditional OOH with better targeting
- Integration with other digital channels (omnichannel)

## Experiment Plan

### 5 Experiment Charters

| # | Experiment | Type | Duration | Cost | Evidence |
|---|------------|------|----------|------|----------|
| 1 | Media Buyer Feature Priority Survey | Discovery Survey | 2 weeks | $0 | Weak |
| 2 | Agency Decision-Maker Interviews | Customer Interviews | 3 weeks | $500 | Medium |
| 3 | XanhSM Partnership LOI | Letter of Intent | 4 weeks | $0 | Very Strong |
| 4 | CPM Pricing Pre-Sale Test | Pre-sale | 3 weeks | $1,200 | Very Strong |
| 5 | DSP Integration Feasibility Spike | XP Spike | 4 weeks | $2,000 | Strong |

### Experiment Sequencing

```
WEEK 1-2: Foundation (Parallel)
├─ #1: Media Buyer Survey (FREE)
├─ #3: XanhSM LOI Negotiation (starts Week 1)

WEEK 3-4: Demand Validation
└─ #2: Agency Interviews [depends on #1]

WEEK 5-7: Monetization Proof
└─ #4: Pre-Sales [depends on #2 + #3]

WEEK 6-9: Technical Feasibility (Parallel with #4)
└─ #5: DSP Integration Spike

TOTAL TIMELINE: 9 weeks
TOTAL BUDGET: $3,500 - $4,800
```

### Decision Gates

| After | If | Then |
|-------|-----|------|
| Experiment #1 | <50% care about real-time data | Pivot interview script to winning feature |
| Experiment #2 | <30% interested in pilots | STOP, revisit value proposition |
| Experiment #3 | LOI fails | Pause pre-sales, find alternative partner |
| Experiment #4 | <$100K pre-sold | Reduce scope, only 1 DSP integration |
| Experiment #5 | DSP infeasible in 6 months | Pivot to self-serve dashboard |
