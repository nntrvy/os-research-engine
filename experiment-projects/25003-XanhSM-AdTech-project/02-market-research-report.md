### Critical Gap

> **No verified programmatic taxi advertising service exists in Vietnam** despite strong market conditions.

### Global Benchmarks

| Market | Company | Implementation | Scale | Lesson |
|--------|---------|----------------|-------|--------|
| **NYC** | Screenverse + SOMO | Digital taxi tops | 4,000 screens | Launched Oct 2024, 3B monthly impressions |
| **NYC** | Vistar/Hivestack | Programmatic integration | 13K taxis | Geo-targeted, real-time bidding |
| **Singapore** | JCDecaux | Changi Airport programmatic | 191 screens | 120M monthly impressions |
| **Thailand** | Plan B + Vistar | 7-Eleven screens | 500+ screens | Premium retail network |

**Vietnam Advantage:** 100K+ taxis vs NYC's 13K = larger potential fleet.

## Key Success Factors

1. **XanhSM Exclusivity:** 100,000-vehicle fleet = largest DOOH taxi inventory in SEA
2. **First-Mover Advantage:** No programmatic competitor in Vietnam
3. **Proven Global Playbook:** NYC, Singapore models validated
4. **Measurable ROI:** Addresses Vietnam's fragmented OOH measurement
5. **Privacy-Compliant:** Anonymized GPS data, no third-party cookies
