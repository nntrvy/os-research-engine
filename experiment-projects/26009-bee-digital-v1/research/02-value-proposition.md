## Three-Sided Value Proposition

### 1. For EV Brands

> "Delight your customers. Embed better insurance."

**Pain Points**:
- Insurance is fragmented, hard to integrate, hurts UX
- Managing insurers and regulations is complex and time-consuming
- Poor claims experience damages brand reputation and trust

**Solutions**:
1. Embedded insurance SDK/API for companion, rental, and taxi apps
2. White-label UI for purchase, management, and claims
3. Integration layer with BeeVN for compliance and insurer operations

**Gains**:
1. Remove friction of insurance as separate process
2. Unlock best coverage & pricing through insurer partnerships
3. Increase revenue while adding customer value

---

### 2. For BeeVN (Broker)

> "Tech infrastructure so you can focus on compliance & insurer ops."

**Products & Services**:
- White-label platform with APIs, dashboards, claims, policy & payment modules
- Customizable workflows & automated reporting
- Partner onboarding toolkit
- Secure customer data infrastructure

**Pain Relievers**:
- Reduce manual work via automation (claims, underwriting, data entry)
- Simplify compliance reporting with built-in logs & audit trails
- Faster onboarding for new insurers

**Gain Creators**:
- BeeVN becomes "tech-enabled" broker without in-house dev team
- Serve more insurers & EV clients with less effort
- Stronger value proposition to insurers = more deals

---

### 3. For Insurers

> "Access high-quality EV customers via embedded channels + better data."

**Products & Services**:
- Embedded insurance APIs
- Behavioral & transactional customer insights
- Customizable policy engine
- Real-time claims & fraud prevention tools

**Pain Relievers**:
- Reduce CAC through embedded channels
- Lower risk via data-driven underwriting
- Improve NPS with seamless digital claims

**Gain Creators**:
- Tap into high-LTV EV customers
- Launch contextual products (low-mileage, driver protection, etc.)
- Position as modern insurer via co-branding in trusted EV apps
