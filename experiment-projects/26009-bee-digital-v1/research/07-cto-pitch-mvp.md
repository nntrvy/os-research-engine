# 07 - CTO Pitch: AI Car Damage Inspector MVP

**Date**: Aug 29, 2025

**Context**: Looking for technical co-founder. Considering alpaca.vn (core insurance platform builder with Ravin.AI partnership).

---

## Product Concept

**Electrik - Nhanh như chớp** (Fast as lightning)

> App giám định thiệt hại & hỗ trợ bồi thường bảo hiểm dành cho xe điện.
>
> An AI-powered mobile app that lets EV drivers scan damages, file claims, and get routed to the nearest available garage.

---

## The Problem

EV owners in Vietnam (starting with VinFast) face delays after accidents:

1. Manual inspections slow down insurance claims
2. Drivers don't know the nearest available garage
3. Insurer processes lack transparency

---

## The Solution

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI Damage Detection** | Dents, scratches, cracks, misalignments |
| **Severity & Repair Suggestion** | Minor (buff), Moderate (repaint), Severe (replace) |
| **Smart Routing** | Connects driver to nearest garage with shortest queue |
| **Insurance Sync** | Generates standardized claim reports for partner insurers |
| **EV-Ready Expansion** | Built to embed inside VinFast apps |

### Technical Approach

- Works on **any smartphone** (AI from photos/video)
- **LiDAR-enhanced** if device supports it
- No special hardware required

### Use Cases

1. **Post-accident inspection** - giám định sau khi xảy ra tai nạn
2. **Rental car inspection** - giám định xe trước và sau khi thuê xe

---
