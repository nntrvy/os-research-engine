## Insurance Fraud - 5 Common Types (Vietnam)

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Falsify loss timing** | Claim for damage that occurred before policy purchase |
| **2. Double-dip claims** | Already compensated by at-fault third party, but create fake scene to claim from insurer again |
| **3. Hide violations** | No license or under influence, but lie to receive compensation |
| **4. Inflate loss value** | Intentionally replace old/broken parts, stage scene to legitimize |
| **5. Cross-border fraud** | Damage outside Vietnam without rider, drag car back to Vietnam, report fake accident location |

*Source: Thời Báo Tài Chính*

## Industry Direction

According to experts, to maintain efficiency and sustainable development, insurers are accelerating:

1. **Digital tech for damage assessment**
2. **Fraud detection systems**
3. **Cost management process optimization**

These are key factors to improve service quality and build trust with motor insurance customers.

**Additional need**: Insurers must re-establish premium pricing methods and seriously implement a **shared database**.

## Competitive Landscape Clarification

### Fermion/Merimen

**Status**: NOT a direct competitor

**Reality**: Old-timer in the market, one of the established players providing claims infrastructure to insurers. They are part of the ecosystem, not competing in the same space Electrik is targeting.

**Electrik's differentiation**: Customer-centric, EV-specialized, end-to-end solution vs. Merimen's insurer-centric B2B platform.
