## How to Use This Profile

This Customer Profile represents VinFast Aftersales as a customer segment.
To find solution fit, create a **Value Map** (VPC left side) that addresses:

1. **Pain Relievers** — How do we solve fragmented data, DMS gaps, and insurer disconnection?
2. **Gain Creators** — How do we enable centralized tracking, easy insurer connectivity, and blocker accountability?
3. **Products & Services** — What specific features/tools address these jobs?

The Value Map can come from Bee-Digital, OSR, or any other solution provider.
