## Appendix: Detailed VPC & BMC

### Value Proposition Description

**Electrik** is a technology platform supporting motor vehicle insurance claims processing that is fast, transparent, and efficient.

**For Car Owners**:
- Complete entire process in VinFast app or on EV HUD screen
- Unified experience across 7 registered insurers
- **10x faster**: Small claims in 4 hours, complex in 48 hours
- **Precondition**: Customer agrees to share data (documents, photos, vehicle history) for AI auto-verification
- **Result**: 90% time savings, fair treatment, restored trust

**For Repair Workshops**:
- Solves biggest problem: slow insurance payments + complex manual process
- All steps online via dedicated dashboard: quotes → approval → payment
- Transparent comparison between workshop-insurer-customer
- Eliminates hidden costs, reduces disputes
- **Result**: On-time payments, stable cash flow, improved reputation

**For Insurers**:
- Currently fraud = ~15% of claims costs
- AI analyzes: driving behavior, loss history, claim patterns to detect anomalies
- Transparent claims → fast-track processing
- Suspicious claims → detailed inspection
- **Result**: Save hundreds of billions annually, optimize resources, improve loss ratio in 1-2 years
