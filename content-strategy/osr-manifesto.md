**2. The best ideas look stupid at first.**
If everyone gets it immediately, you're probably in a red ocean. Blue oceans feel weird until they're obvious.

**3. Validation > Vision.**
Big visions are cheap. We don't trust ideasâ€”we test them. Structured experiments. Real evidence. Kill what doesn't work. Double down on what does.

**5. AI is our unfair advantage.**
