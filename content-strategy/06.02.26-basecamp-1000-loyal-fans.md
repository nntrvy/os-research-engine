**Posted to Basecamp: 06.02.26**

## The Goal

**1,000 loyal fans by end of 2026.**

Not followers. Not subscribers. Not impressions.

Loyal fans who:
- Subscribe to our newsletter and actually read it
- Watch hours of our behind-the-scenes content
- Tell their friends and family about us
- Want to apply and work with us
- Won't shut up about OS Research at dinner parties

1,000 people like this is worth more than 100,000 casual followers.

## Why This Matters

We're on a mission to create 50 blue oceans. That's ambitious. That's weird. That takes years.

We need people who believe in the mission. People who follow the journey. People who cheer us on when we succeed and stick around when we fail.

These 1,000 fans become our:
- First customers for new ventures
- Source of referrals and introductions
- Recruiting pipeline for talent
- Accountability partners (they'll notice if we go quiet)

## Our Voice

We're not corporate. We're not "thought leaders." We're not polished to the point of boring.

**We are:**
- Obsessed (about blue oceans, obviously)
- Honest (even when it hurts)
- Visual (show, don't tell)
- Human (we fuck up and we share it)

**The vibe:** Anthony Bourdain meets startup documentary. Explorers hunting for something most people can't see.

- Every Friday
- 5-minute read
