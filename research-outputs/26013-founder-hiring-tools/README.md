# 26013: Founder Hiring Tools - Market Analysis

**Project:** Hiring Automation for Solo Founders and Small Startup Teams
**Type:** Competitive Intelligence + Market Gap Analysis
**Status:** Initial Research Complete
**Created:** 14.03.26

---

## Overview

Research scope: Identify existing solutions that address the pain point of solo founders and small startup teams (under 10 people) wasting excessive time on hiring processes. Includes AI recruiting tools, ATS systems, fractional recruiting services, job boards with screening, and autonomous AI recruiters.

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| Market is crowded at the tool level (ATS, job boards) but most tools are built for HR teams, not founders | Founders are not the true design target of most existing products |
| Dover is the closest match (free ATS + fractional recruiter marketplace) but primarily serves US-based tech startups | Geographic gap exists for Vietnam/SEA |
| Tezi.ai is the most aggressive "founder-first" autonomous recruiter but pricing is $1K-$10K/year and US-centric | Pricing and geography gap for emerging markets |
| Vietnam market: VietnamWorks and TopCV dominate job distribution, with basic AI; no lightweight founder-oriented tool exists locally | Clear local gap |
| The true unsolved problem is not ATS features - it is the cognitive overhead of running a hiring process with no dedicated person | Most tools assume you have someone whose job is recruiting |
| No product in the market combines: (1) job description drafting, (2) multi-board posting, (3) async AI screening, (4) scheduling, in a single flow designed for a non-recruiter founder | Whitespace in workflow design |
