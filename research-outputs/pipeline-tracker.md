---

## Stage Definitions

| Stage | Description | Criteria | Budget |
|-------|-------------|----------|--------|
| **Idea** | Problem hunting, market research, social listening | Raw research, no validated hypotheses | Research time only |
| **Project** | Ready to run experiments and validate | Has VPC/BMC, hypotheses, experiment designs | Up to 50M VND |
| **Venture** | Validated problem, building/testing solution | MVP, validated desirability, active experiments | Up to 3B VND |
| **Spin-off** | PMF achieved, ready to raise external capital | Repeatable revenue, positive unit economics | Raise external funding |

## IDEA Stage (4 projects)

### 26013 — Founder Hiring Tools
- **Focus:** Solo founders waste time on hiring; no tool treats them as core persona
- **Artifacts:** Competitive landscape (20+ tools analyzed), gap analysis
- **Next:** VPC/BMC, hypotheses, existing solutions deep-dive
- **Key Insight:** Per-hire pricing ($99-199) vs monthly subscriptions; Vietnam job board integration gap

### 26012-r — MVNO Vietnam
- **Focus:** Vietnam MVNO market sizing and competitive landscape
- **Artifacts:** Market research (TAM $5.2B, SAM $1.56B, SOM $100-120M)
- **Next:** Identify specific pain point, customer segment, VPC
- **Key Insight:** 2.1% market share, 13.3% CAGR — growing but unclear OSR angle

### 26008 — Speech Practice Studio
- **Focus:** "Private speech gym" — soundproof booths + AI for movie scene practice
- **Artifacts:** Idea refinement doc, basic VPC
- **Next:** Customer interviews, willingness-to-pay validation
- **Key Insight:** No direct competitor with this combination; targets introverted professionals 30+

### 26011 — VinFast Insurance Markets
- **Focus:** Regulatory analysis for BeeVN expansion (DRC/Congo, Kazakhstan, Philippines)
- **Artifacts:** Three-market regulatory comparison, capital requirements
- **Next:** Client decision on which market to enter
- **Key Insight:** Policy research for client, not a standalone venture

## PROJECT Stage — Ready to Run Tests (4 projects)

### 26007 — Children Age 2-10 (AI Child Development)
- **Focus:** AI-powered platform for developmental disorders (autism, ADHD)
- **Artifacts:** VPC/BMC, market research, business pitch
- **Market:** TAM 2.5M children; monthly family spend 8.5-11.5M VND
- **Next:** Design experiments, customer interviews with parents
- **Blue Ocean:** Affordable AI-guided home intervention (vs expensive therapy centers)

### 26005 — AI Disability & Accessibility
- **Focus:** Vietnamese Sign Language (VSL) app + hospital accessibility
- **Artifacts:** v2 research, 4 hypotheses (H1-H4), 18 risks identified, who-pays analysis
- **Market:** Global TAM $25-36B; realistic SOM $3M; only 10 VSL interpreters nationwide
- **Next:** Run H1 experiment (VSL app feasibility), NGO partnership outreach
- **Model:** B2G via NGO partnerships

### 26006 — Reintegration Platform
- **Focus:** Employment + support platform for ex-prisoners and recovering addicts
- **Artifacts:** 5 experiment designs, budget calculated (25-35M VND, 10 weeks)
- **Market:** 45K-55K people/year; 85-90% relapse rate; $928M govt spend 2025-2030
- **Next:** Execute Experiment 1 (SME employer interviews)
- **Model:** B2G leveraging existing government budget

### 26010 — Millennial Dad Bonding
- **Focus:** Pivoted to parent platform with mom-as-buyer (controls 85% household spend)
- **Artifacts:** VPC/BMC v2, 9 hypotheses (3 desirability, 2 feasibility, 3 viability), 5 competitor analyses
- **Market:** TAM expanded $50M → $6B (SEA parents 0-5); B2B2C via corporate benefits
- **Next:** Phase 1 validation (10 mom interviews, 10 dad interviews, landing page, HR interviews)
- **Post-VC Roast:** Refined after tough critique; strongest research package in pipeline
