# 26008: Speech Practice Studio

**Project:** Private Speech Gym with AI Feedback
**Type:** Idea Refinement
**Status:** Idea Stage - Needs Validation
**Created:** 03.02.26

---

## Overview

A "private speech gym" combining soundproof practice booths with an AI-powered app for gamified movie scene practice. Targets introverts and professionals who avoid speaking practice due to fear of judgment.

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| "Humanless + judgment-free" is unique positioning | Toastmasters/coaches can't offer this |
| Target: professionals 30+ avoiding public speaking | Fear of judgment is the blocker |
| Gamification via movie scenes | Fun > boring drills |
| No direct competitor with this combination | Blue ocean opportunity |

---

## Value Proposition

Network of soundproof practice studios with AI-powered mobile app to help introverted professionals overcome speaking anxiety through gamified movie scene practice.
