## Opportunity Areas from Facebook Data

1. **Independent Insurance Review Platform**
   - Vietnamese consumers actively seek reviews before buying
   - No trusted neutral platform exists
   - Facebook groups fill this gap informally

2. **Insurance Dispute Resolution Service**
   - 3+ month waits for simple refunds
   - Legal recourse is expensive and slow
   - Mediation service opportunity

3. **Contract Comparison Tool**
   - Electronic vs paper contract discrepancies
   - Hidden clauses in 100-page contracts
   - AI-powered contract analysis

4. **Bancassurance Transparency**
   - Bank employees incentivized to mis-sell
   - Clear separation of banking vs insurance needed
   - Education content opportunity
