## Overview

Research on AI-powered child development platform targeting children ages 2-10 with developmental disorders (autism, ADHD, developmental delays) and their caregivers in Vietnam.

---

## Key Findings

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Autism prevalence | 1-2% (120K-240K children) |
| ADHD prevalence | 3.2-9.3% (600K children) |
| Combined TAM | ~2.5M children with support needs |
| Monthly family spend | 8.5-11.5M VND ($340-460) |
| Insurance coverage | NONE for autism/ADHD |
| Sign language interpreters | Only 10 nationwide |

**Blue Ocean Space:** Affordable, AI-guided home intervention platform that democratizes access to evidence-based therapy.
