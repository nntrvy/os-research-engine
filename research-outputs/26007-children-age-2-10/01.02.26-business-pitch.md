## 1. PROBLEM

### The 18-Month Gap: Where Parents Have Zero Solutions

**Every Vietnamese parent worries:** "Is my child developing normally?"

At 18 months, parents notice delays. But here's the problem:

| Stage | What Happens | Solution Available |
|-------|--------------|-------------------|
| **0-18 months** | Parents worry, search Google | NOTHING — just anxiety |
| **18-24 months** | Parents seek diagnosis | Months of waiting for appointment |
| **24-36 months** | Finally diagnosed | Centers have 6-12 month waitlists |
| **3+ years** | Start intervention | Golden window (before age 3) already MISSED |

**The 18-month gap costs families everything:**
- 200M+ VND lifetime center costs
- School rejection trauma ("kicked from 10+ schools")
- Parents "drowning in billions VND debt" to save their children
- Marriages collapse from financial and emotional stress

### Market Reality

| Segment | Size | Current Solution |
|---------|------|------------------|
| **All children 2-10** | 12 million | None (parents guess) |
| **Children with developmental concerns** | 2.5 million | Centers: 5-15M VND/month (unaffordable) |
| **Diagnosed autism/ADHD** | 720,000 | Waitlists, inconsistent quality |

**The insight:** 80% of children with delays are UNDIAGNOSED. Parents are searching at 18 months but no one serves them until age 3+.

### Priority 1: EXPERIMENT (Test Immediately)

#### H1: Universal Market Appeal [DESIRABILITY - CRITICAL]
**We believe that** "smart development tracking for every child" messaging WILL convert 5-10x better than "autism intervention" messaging, attracting parents at 18 months instead of post-diagnosis.

- **If wrong:** We're stuck with the smaller 2.5M market
- **Test:** A/B landing pages: "Theo dõi phát triển thông minh cho mọi trẻ" vs. "Can thiệp trẻ tự kỷ"
- **Success criteria:** Universal messaging converts 3x+ better

---

#### H2: 18-Month Entry Point [DESIRABILITY - CRITICAL]
**We believe that** worried parents at 18 months WILL pay 250K/month for AI development tracking even without a diagnosis, because the alternative is 18 months of anxiety with zero solutions.

- **If wrong:** Must wait until diagnosis (lose 18-month window)
- **Test:** Pre-sales to parents in "worried" stage via Facebook groups
- **Success criteria:** 100 signups at 250K/month within 30 days

---

#### H3: Daily Habit Formation [DESIRABILITY - CRITICAL]
**We believe that** parents WILL complete 10-15 min daily activities at 70%+ consistency (5+ days/week) for 4+ weeks when gamification (streaks, badges) and AI progress tracking are included.

- **If wrong:** Core intervention model doesn't work
- **Test:** Concierge with 30 families using Zalo daily reminders
- **Success criteria:** 60%+ complete 5+ activities/week for 4 weeks

---

#### H4: Outcome Guarantee Conversion [VIABILITY - CRITICAL]
**We believe that** a "3-month improvement guarantee or full refund" WILL increase conversion by 50%+ because it de-risks the purchase decision for skeptical parents.

- **If wrong:** Standard conversion rates, no differentiation
- **Test:** A/B pricing page with/without guarantee
- **Success criteria:** Guarantee version converts 30%+ better

---

#### H5: Tier Upsell Path [VIABILITY - CRITICAL]
**We believe that** 40%+ of Early Warning tier users WILL upgrade to Intervention tier when delays are detected, because the AI creates urgency and the price differential (250K → 750K) is affordable.

- **If wrong:** Revenue stuck at low tier, unit economics break
- **Test:** Track upgrade rates in beta cohort
- **Success criteria:** 30%+ upgrade within 6 months
