## Executive Summaries by Country

### DRC (Democratic Republic of Congo)
**Vinfast Interest:** Battery factory operations

**Key Points:**
- DRC is NOT a CIMA member - operates independent regulatory system under ARCA
- Foreign brokers must incorporate as Congolese societe anonyme
- **CRITICAL:** Import cargo insurance MUST be placed with DRC-based insurers
- Market liberalized in 2018; premiums grew 5x to $350M by 2025
- Political violence caused 5-10x premium spikes in 2025 (eastern conflict)
- 10 insurance companies, 12 licensed brokers currently operating
- Major players: SFA, Rawsur, SONAS (state-owned), Activa, Mayfair

**Risk Alert:** No specific environmental liability insurance mandate found despite environmental laws

---

### Kazakhstan
**Vinfast Interest:** Market expansion (details unclear)

**Key Points:**
- Well-regulated market under ARDFM/National Bank of Kazakhstan
- Foreign brokers can establish via branch (with home country confirmation) or local subsidiary
- EAEU membership does NOT provide automatic license recognition
- Strong market growth: +24% premiums in 2025 (KZT 674.5 billion)
- 19,000+ EVs registered by H1 2025; government incentives until end 2025
- Top insurers: IC Centras ($67.8M), Eurasia Insurance ($46.7M), Halyk Insurance ($31.1M)
- New bonus-malus system (18 classes) implemented April 2025

**Opportunity:** EV insurance market underdeveloped - white space for specialization

---

### Philippines
**Vinfast Interest:** Active dealership network (opened July 2024)

**Key Points:**
- Comprehensive Insurance Code framework (PD 612 / RA 10607)
- 100% foreign ownership of brokers permitted (RA 10881)
- Capital requirement: PHP 20M minimum for insurance broker
- Non-life market: USD 2.11 billion (2024), 7.3% CAGR projected
- CTPL minimum coverage increased to PHP 200,000 (2024)
- EVIDA Law (RA 11697) provides EV incentives; 5% fleet mandate for companies
- 80% EV sales growth in 2024; 20,000+ registered vehicles
- Vinfast has BDO partnership including insurance services

**Opportunity:** Position as leading EV insurance broker; capture fleet/TNVS market through PUV modernization

## Foreign Broker Entry Requirements

### DRC
1. Incorporate as **societe anonyme** under Congolese law
2. Appoint qualified management (university degree + 10 years experience)
3. Obtain ARCA authorization as insurance intermediary
4. Establish physical presence in DRC
5. Unknown: Local partnership requirements, specific capital requirements

### Kazakhstan
1. **Option A - Branch:** Submit application via elicense.kz with:
   - Home country regulator confirmation (no criminal record, valid license)
   - Comply with Article 6-2 registration requirements
   - Pay licensing fee
2. **Option B - Subsidiary:** Establish LLP (min 1 partner, USD 1 capital) or JSC
3. Professional indemnity insurance required (as of Jan 2025)

### Philippines
1. Meet PHP 20M minimum paid-up capital
2. Maintain PHP 20M net worth
3. Appoint qualified soliciting official
4. File with Insurance Commission
5. Renew every 3 years
6. No foreign ownership restrictions (100% allowed)
