## Overview

Market entry assessment for BeeVN (Vinfast Authorized Insurance Broker) covering insurance brokerage operations in three markets where Vinfast operates: DRC (Congo), Kazakhstan, and Philippines.

---

## Key Findings

| Factor | DRC (Congo) | Kazakhstan | Philippines |
|--------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| Regulatory Body | ARCA | ARDFM (under NBK) | Insurance Commission |
| Foreign Broker Entry | Local incorporation required | Branch or subsidiary | 100% ownership allowed |
| Capital Requirement | Not specified | Varies by form | PHP 20M (~$350K) |
| Compulsory Motor | Third-party liability | OSAGO | CTPL (PHP 200K min) |
| EV Market Maturity | Minimal | Growing (19K+ EVs) | Emerging (20K+ EVs) |
| Vinfast Operations | Battery factory | Expansion planned | Active dealerships |
