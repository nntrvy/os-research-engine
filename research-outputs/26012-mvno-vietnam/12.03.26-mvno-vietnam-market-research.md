### Growth Drivers

- **5G expansion**: 32.5% CAGR for 5G MVNO segment
- **MNP regulations** (Aug 2025): Reduces switching friction
- **2G/3G sunset**: 2G ends 2026, 3G ends 2028 → forces migration to higher-ARPU plans
- **Digital-only models**: 52.64% market share, 7.95% CAGR
- **LOT 2023 law**: Mandates MNOs to lease wholesale capacity to MVNOs

## 6. TAM / SAM / SOM Analysis

### Definitions for MVNO Market

```
TAM = Total Addressable Market (all potential mobile users)
SAM = Serviceable Addressable Market (realistic target segments)
SOM = Serviceable Obtainable Market (achievable market share)
```

### TAM (Total Addressable Market)

| Component | Calculation |
|-----------|-------------|
| Vietnam mobile subscriptions | 124.64 million |
| Average ARPU (blended) | ~$3.50/month |
| **Annual TAM** | **~$5.2 billion/year** |

Alternative view:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total telecom services revenue Vietnam | ~$7-8 billion/year |
| Mobile services portion | ~65% = **$4.5-5.2B** |

### SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)

MVNOs typically target price-sensitive segments:

| Target Segment | Population | % of Market |
|----------------|------------|-------------|
| Students | ~15 million | 12% |
| Low-income workers | ~25 million | 20% |
| Factory workers | ~10 million | 8% |
| Gig economy workers | ~5 million | 4% |
| Price-sensitive switchers | ~10 million | 8% |
| **Total SAM users** | **~65 million** | **52%** |

| SAM Calculation | Value |
|-----------------|-------|
| SAM subscribers | 65 million |
| Lower ARPU (MVNO avg) | ~$2.00/month |
| **Annual SAM** | **~$1.56 billion/year** |

### SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)

Realistic MVNO capture over 5 years:

| Scenario | Market Penetration | Subscribers | Annual Revenue |
|----------|-------------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Conservative (Current)** | 2.1% of total | 2.65M | ~$25M |
| **Moderate (2027)** | 5% of total | 6.2M | ~$60M |
| **Optimistic (2030)** | 8-10% of total | 10-12M | ~$100-120M |

## 7. Market Opportunity Summary

### Why MVNO Market is Attractive

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| 13.3% CAGR to 2030 | High growth vs mature MNO market |
| MNP regulations (Aug 2025) | Lower switching barriers |
| 2G/3G sunset | Forces migration, new customer acquisition opportunity |
| LOT 2023 | Regulatory support for infrastructure sharing |
| Low current penetration (2.1%) | Massive headroom vs global avg (10-15%) |
| Fintech bundling | New revenue streams beyond connectivity |

### Key Success Factors

1. **Ecosystem integration** (Masan, VNPay, FPT models)
2. **Distribution leverage** (retail, digital, partnerships)
3. **Niche targeting** (not competing head-on with MNOs)
4. **Digital-first operations** (low OPEX)
5. **Value-added services** (fintech, content, IoT)
