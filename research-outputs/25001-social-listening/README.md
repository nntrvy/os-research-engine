## Overview

Social listening research analyzing Vietnamese consumer attitudes and pain points regarding life insurance products. Data gathered from VozForums, Facebook groups, and Vietnamese news sites.

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Implication |
|---------|-------------|
| Deep distrust due to deceptive sales practices | Trust-building is critical for any insurance product |
| Contracts 70-100 pages, incomprehensible | Simplification is a blue ocean opportunity |
| 60-74 year maturity dates common | Consumers feel trapped in unreasonable commitments |
| Only 50-85% return on early cancellation | High switching costs create resentment |
| "Savings deposits converted to insurance" fraud | Bank-insurance channel has credibility crisis |
