## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Original Hypothesis:** Millennial dads want help bonding with their 0-5 year old sons

**Refined Hypothesis (Post-VC Roast):** Early childhood development platform that REQUIRES father involvement, solving mom burnout while improving child outcomes. Sold through B2B2C (corporations, insurers) with moms as primary buyer in D2C channel.

**Market Opportunity:**
- Original TAM: ~$50M (Vietnam affluent dads with sons)
- Expanded TAM: ~$6B (Southeast Asia millennial parents, all children 0-5)
- Category: Early Childhood Development + Parental Engagement

## PART 1: CUSTOMER PROFILES

### Profile A: PRIMARY BUYER - "The Overwhelmed Mom"
> **Role:** Decision maker, purchaser (85% of household spending)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Demographics** | Millennial female (28-42), urban professional, child 0-5 |
| **Situation** | Drowning in unequal parenting load, wants dad more involved |
| **Core JTBD** | "Help me get my partner more involved WITHOUT nagging" |

**Functional Jobs:**
- Get partner to take on more childcare responsibilities
- Reduce her cognitive load and mental burden
- Ensure child hits developmental milestones

**Emotional Jobs:**
- Feel less alone in parenting
- Reduce resentment toward partner
- Feel confident child is developing well

**Social Jobs:**
- Be seen as having an equal partnership
- Not be "the nagging wife"
- Have a happy, thriving family

---

### Profile B: PRIMARY USER - "The Present Dad"
> **Role:** End user, beneficiary

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Demographics** | Millennial male (28-42), urban professional, child 0-5 |
| **Situation** | Wants to bond but lacks confidence/guidance, feels "dad guilt" |
| **Core JTBD** | "Help me feel competent and connected as a father" |

**Functional Jobs:**
- Know what activities to do at each developmental stage
- Have structured time with child that "counts"
- Build skills to handle tantrums, emotions, behaviors

**Emotional Jobs:**
- Feel like a competent, confident father (not secondary parent)
- Overcome guilt from working long hours
- Be different from his own emotionally distant father

**Social Jobs:**
- Be seen as an involved, modern dad
- Earn respect from partner
- Model healthy masculinity for child

---

### Profile C: CORPORATE BUYER - "HR Benefits Manager"
> **Role:** B2B purchaser, budget holder

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Demographics** | HR/People Ops leader at company with 500+ employees |
| **Situation** | Losing talent after parental leave, low utilization of benefits |
| **Core JTBD** | "Help me retain working parents and improve DEI metrics" |

**Functional Jobs:**
- Reduce post-parental-leave attrition (currently 40%+ leave within 12 months)
- Increase paternity leave utilization
- Demonstrate ROI on benefits spend

**Emotional Jobs:**
- Feel like a progressive, people-first company
- Reduce guilt about demanding work culture

**Social Jobs:**
- Be recognized as "best place for working parents"
- Win employer brand awards

---

### Profile D: INFLUENCER - "Pediatrician / Child Dev Expert"
> **Role:** Trusted authority, endorser

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Core JTBD** | "Help me give parents evidence-based guidance" |

**What They Validate:**
- Father involvement improves developmental outcomes
- Early bonding creates secure attachment
- Structured activities support milestone achievement

---

### Profile E: POTENTIAL SABOTEUR - "The Gatekeeper Mom"
> **Role:** Can block dad's involvement (unconsciously)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Behavior** | Maternal gatekeeping - correcting dad, taking over, not creating space |
| **Mitigation** | Position product as MOM's tool to enable dad, not compete with her |

## PART 7: RISKS & MITIGATIONS

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Dads don't engage with app-based activities | Medium | High | Gamification, partner accountability, physical kit option |
| Mom gatekeeping blocks dad involvement | Medium | Medium | Position as "mom's tool to enable dad" |
| Corporate sales cycle too long | High | Medium | Start with SMBs, build case studies |
| Content creation costs spiral | Medium | High | Curate + license, don't create |
| Competitors copy model | Low | Medium | First-mover advantage, community moat |
