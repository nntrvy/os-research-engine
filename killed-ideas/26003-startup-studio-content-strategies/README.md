## Overview

Analysis of content strategies from 6 leading startup studios and VCs: 37Signals, Human Ventures, Earlybird VC, Science Inc, High Alpha, and Switch VC. Identifies patterns for OS Research content strategy.

---

## Key Findings

| Pattern | Description | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Founder-led authenticity | Personal voice > corporate tone | 37Signals (Jason Fried) |
| Open-source knowledge | Share proprietary frameworks freely | Ruby on Rails, Shape Up |
| Long-form educational | Deep content > clickbait | Bird's View blog |
| Content as byproduct | Document work, don't manufacture | Human Ventures |
| Consistency > virality | 25+ years of blogging | Signal v. Noise |
