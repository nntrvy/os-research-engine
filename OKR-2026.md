## "Validated" Definition

A venture is **validated** when it meets **≥4 of 5 criteria**:

| # | Criteria | Threshold |
|---|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Funding** | 3B VND seed secured from external investor |
| 2 | **PMF Score** | ≥40% "very disappointed" (Sean Ellis Test) |
| 3 | **Customers** | ≥5 paying customers or signed LOIs |
| 4 | **Unit Economics** | LTV:CAC ≥2:1 |
| 5 | **Retention** | ≥70% monthly retention |
