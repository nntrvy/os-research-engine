## Decisions Made

### Strategic Decisions

1. **Expand Target Market:** From affluent Vietnam dads with sons → all Southeast Asia millennial parents with children 0-5 (any gender)
2. **Position Mom as Buyer:** Recognize that moms control 85% of household spending and are desperate for solution to unequal parenting load
3. **Add B2B2C Channel:** Corporate HR and health insurers as primary revenue (60%), D2C as secondary (30%)
4. **Focus on Outcomes, Not Activities:** Position as "early childhood development platform" not "dad activities app"
5. **Curate, Don't Create:** Partner with pediatricians and child development experts for content, don't build from scratch

### Framework Decisions

1. **North Star Metric:** Dad Engagement Hours/Week (measures actual behavior change)
2. **Test Priority:** Desirability first (mom willingness to pay, dad engagement), then Feasibility (content curation), then Viability (corporate pilots)
3. **Risk Mitigation Focus:** Address maternal gatekeeping by positioning product as "mom's tool to enable dad"
