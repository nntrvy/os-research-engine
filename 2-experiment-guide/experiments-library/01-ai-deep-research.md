## Purpose

Use AI to conduct efficient data collection at lower cost as a starting point for market research.

**âœ… Ideal for:** Starting point for any research, giving you a comprehensive overview at low cost

**âŒ Not ideal for:** Making important decisions based solely on AI research - human-to-human connection remains the best approach for in-depth insights

## Preparation

1. **Select AI platform** (Perplexity or ChatGPT)
2. **List market hypotheses** you want to explore:
   - **Industry forces:** competitors, existing solutions, emerging players
   - **Market forces:** customer segments, needs and demands, revenue attractiveness
   - **Key trends:** technology trends, regulatory environment, socio-cultural trends
   - **Macroeconomic forces:** economic situation and infrastructure
3. **Create your prompt** (see prompt templates in comments)

## Execution

- Ask questions and wait for AI response (approximately 15 minutes)

## Analysis

- Collect your results
- Consider focusing on **Problem Size vs Market Size**
  - What problem has the highest search volume?
  - Is this a meaningful business opportunity for you?
- Research attached sources and select the most credible ones
- Synthesize the 3 most important lessons:
  - Market size & opportunities
  - Best potential customer segments
  - Should we continue pursuing this project?
