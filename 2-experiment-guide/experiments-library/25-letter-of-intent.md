## Purpose

A written document expressing serious interest or commitment from a potential customer or partner to engage with your solution when it becomes available.

**âœ… Ideal for:** Validating strong interest from B2B customers or key partners before building

## Preparation

1. **Identify target customers or partners** who would benefit from your solution
2. **Create a presentation or proposal** explaining your value proposition
3. **Draft a Letter of Intent template** that captures commitment level
4. **Schedule meetings** with decision-makers

## Execution

1. **Present your solution** to potential customers/partners
2. **Answer questions** and address concerns
3. **Request Letter of Intent** if they express interest
4. **Document feedback** regardless of outcome

## Analysis

- How many meetings resulted in signed LOIs?
- What objections came up repeatedly?
- What terms or conditions do customers want?
- Is there enough demand to justify building?

## Evidence Strength

âš¡ **Strong** (4/5)

### Signed LOI - Strong Evidence
- Written commitment from decision-maker
- Shows serious intent to purchase
- Validates willingness to pay

### Verbal Commitment - Medium Evidence
- Strong interest but no documentation
- Less reliable than written commitment

### Meeting Feedback - Weak Evidence
- Useful insights but no commitment

## Skills Required

- Sales / Business Development / Negotiation

Ability to present compelling value proposition and ask for commitment.

## Requirements

- Clear value proposition
- Target customer list
- Presentation materials
- LOI template
