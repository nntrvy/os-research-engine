## Purpose

Similar to customer interviews, but focused on discovering whether it's feasible to deploy your business. You'll source and interview key partners to complement Key Activities and Key Resources that you can't or don't want to do in-house.

**âœ… Ideal for:** Validating feasibility by understanding what partners can provide and at what cost

## Preparation

1. **Identify key partners** needed for your business model
2. **Write interview script** focused on:
   - Their capabilities and capacity
   - Pricing and terms
   - Timeline for engagement
   - Past experience with similar partnerships

3. **Source potential partners**
4. **Schedule interviews**

## Execution

1. **Conduct interviews** using your script
2. **Take detailed notes** on capabilities, pricing, and terms
3. **Request proposals or quotes** when appropriate
4. **Follow up** with clarifying questions

## Analysis

1. **Compare responses** across partners
2. **Evaluate fit** with your business model
3. **Update Business Model Canvas** Key Partners block
4. **Assess overall feasibility** based on findings

## Questions to Ask Partners

### Capability Questions
- What services/products do you offer?
- What's your production/service capacity?
- What's your typical lead time?
- What quality standards do you maintain?

### Business Questions
- What are your pricing models?
- What are your payment terms?
- What's your minimum order quantity?
- Do you offer exclusivity arrangements?

### Relationship Questions
- How do you typically work with partners like us?
- What does a successful partnership look like?
- What challenges have you faced with similar partnerships?
- Can you provide references?
