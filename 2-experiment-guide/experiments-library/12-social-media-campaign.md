## Purpose

Social media messages deployed over a specific time period to customers.

**âœ… Ideal for:** Acquiring new customers, increasing brand loyalty, and driving sales

## Preparation

1. **Define campaign objectives**
2. **Identify platforms** to use
3. **Create editorial calendar** and posting schedule
4. **Create social media content**

## Execution

1. Post content on platforms according to schedule
2. Monitor, respond, and engage with commenters

## Analysis

Analyze which posts and platforms are performing best:
- What type of content is getting the most shares?
- What type of content is driving the most clicks?
- What type of content is attracting the most comments?
- What type of content is driving the most conversions?

Summarize with your team and decide what modifications to make for the next campaign.

## Cost

ğŸ’° **Low to Medium** (2-3/5)

- Moderately cheap if you do the work yourself and don't pay for social media advertising
- Costs can rise quickly ($5k-$20k per month) if you pay someone to manage and create content

## Time

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Setup | Days to weeks (depending on content volume) |
| Running | Weeks to months |

Setup time increases if running on multiple platforms. Running time includes posting, reading, and responding on social media, plus measuring effectiveness against business goals.

## Evidence Strength

### Engagement - Weak (2/5)
- **Views:** Number of people who saw your posts
- **Shares:** Number of times content was shared
- **Comments:** Feedback and discussions

Engagement is fairly weak evidence. You can learn deep qualitative lessons from comments to inform your value proposition.

### Click-Through Rate - Weak to Medium (2-3/5)
- CTR = views your social media post receives Ã· people who clicked

### Conversion Rate - Strong (4/5)
- Conversion Rate = people who clicked social media link Ã· people who used it to sign up or purchase

Conversions are strong evidence and can help you identify which social media platform works best for driving business.

## Skills Required

- Design / Marketing

**Marketing:** Create, respond, and manage social media across multiple platforms

**Design:** Help shape and visualize content before posting

## Requirements

**Content**

Social media campaigns aren't just about random posting - they're about scheduling posts for many weeks and months.

Without content, your campaign won't succeed. Make sure you have a plan and resources to create content before starting.

- Test different content types
- Analyze and iterate based on data

- Post the same content on all platforms without adapting
- Ignore comments and messages
