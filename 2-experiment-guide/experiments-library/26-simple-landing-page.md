## Purpose

A single-page website focused on communicating your value proposition and capturing interest.

**âœ… Ideal for:** Quickly testing your value proposition with online customers

## Preparation

1. **Define your value proposition** clearly
2. **Create the landing page** with:
   - Compelling headline
   - Clear explanation of the offer
   - Visual representation (image/video)
   - Call-to-action (sign up, learn more, etc.)
3. **Set up analytics** to track visitors and conversions
4. **Plan traffic sources** (ads, social, email)

## Execution

1. **Launch the landing page**
2. **Drive traffic** from various sources
3. **Monitor performance** daily

## Analysis

- How many unique visitors?
- What's the conversion rate (visitors â†’ sign-ups)?
- Which traffic sources convert best?
- What's the bounce rate?
- Use learnings to iterate on messaging and design
