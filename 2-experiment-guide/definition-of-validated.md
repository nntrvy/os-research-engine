# Definition of Validated by OSR

A project is considered **VALIDATED** when it achieves:

```
≥5 instances of Very Strong Evidence (5/5)
```

Each experiment must be at prototype/MVP stage with a maximum budget of **$10,000 USD** (~250M VND).

---

## Evidence Hierarchy

| Level | Rating | Evidence Type |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| Very Weak | 1/5 | Opinions, stated preferences |
| Weak | 2/5 | Interest indicators, engagement |
| Medium | 3/5 | Clicks, sign-ups, time spent |
| Strong | 4/5 | Real commitments, deposits |
| **Very Strong** | **5/5** | **Actual purchases, revenue** |

---

## Experiments That Produce Very Strong Evidence

| Experiment | What Validates | Evidence Source |
|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| [Pre-sale](./experiments-library/16-pre-sale.md) | Willingness to pay | Payment before product exists |
| [Crowdfunding](./experiments-library/18-crowdfunding.md) | Market demand + funding | Money pledged by backers |
| [Concierge](./experiments-library/29-concierge.md) | Service value + pricing | Payment for manual delivery |
| [Wizard of Oz](./experiments-library/30-wizard-of-oz.md) | Product experience | Payment for "automated" service |
| [Single Feature MVP](./experiments-library/32-single-feature-mvp.md) | Core value proposition | Payment + retention |

---

## Validation Distribution

The 5 instances of Very Strong evidence must cover:

| Category | Minimum Required | What It Proves |
|----------|------------------|----------------|
| **Desirability** | 2 | Customers want this solution |
| **Viability** | 2 | Business model works (they will pay) |
| **Feasibility** | 1 | We can deliver at acceptable cost |

---

## Purpose

This validation threshold demonstrates sufficient product-market fit and business model fit to justify a **3 billion VND seed raise** (~$120,000 USD) from VCs, angels, or corporate funds.

The raise is not about needing capital—it is about external validation of the studio thesis and execution capability.

---

## Summary

```
VALIDATED = ≥5 Very Strong Evidence instances
          + ≤$10,000 budget per experiment
          + At least 2 Desirability + 2 Viability + 1 Feasibility
```

*This is the official OSR definition of a validated project.*
