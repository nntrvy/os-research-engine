# OSR Innovation Platform — MLP

> Simple. Lean. Humble.

---

## What This Platform Does

Three things only:

1. **Kill bad ideas fast** — Move from idea to spin-off with evidence gates
2. **Keep what we learn** — Store insights so knowledge stays in the company
3. **Reward people who innovate** — Points become real shares

## The Four Stages

```
IDEA → PROJECT → VENTURE → SPIN-OFF
 │        │          │          │
 ▼        ▼          ▼          ▼
Shape    Test      Build      Fund
(0đ)    (25M)     (200M)     (3B)
```

### Stage 0: Idea
**Budget:** 0 VND
**What happens:** Anyone submits an idea. Quick shaping. Define the problem and who has it.
**Exit criteria:** Pass first betting table. Team says "worth testing."

### Stage 1: Project
**Budget:** Up to 25M VND
**What happens:** Run experiments. Collect evidence. Build confidence.
**Exit criteria:**
- ≥3 experiments completed
- ≥1 strong evidence (real behavior, not just opinions)
- Confidence ≥50%

### Stage 2: Venture
**Budget:** Up to 200M VND
**What happens:** Build MVP. Get paying customers. Prove unit economics.
**Exit criteria:**
- ≥5 paying customers or signed LOIs
- PMF score ≥40% ("very disappointed" if gone)
- LTV:CAC ≥2:1

### Stage 3: Spin-off
**Budget:** 3B VND seed investment
**What happens:** Become a real company. OSR co-invests with external angels.
**Exit criteria:** Meet 4/5 validation criteria from strategic overview.

## The Betting Table

Weekly 30-minute session. Every active project faces 5 questions:

| Question | Pass If |
|----------|---------|
| Is the problem real? | Evidence from real people, not assumptions |
| Is it big enough? | Market size or urgency signals |
| Can we solve it? | Feasibility evidence |
| Will they pay? | Willingness-to-pay signal |
| What's next? | Clear next experiment defined |

**Outcome:**
- ✅ **Pass** → Continue. Unlock more budget if earned.
- ❌ **Kill** → Stop. Capture learnings. Move on.
- 🔄 **Send back** → Not ready. Run specific test first.

---

## Evidence Scoring (Simplified)

Three questions. Score 1-3 each.

| Question | 1 (Weak) | 2 (Medium) | 3 (Strong) |
|----------|----------|------------|------------|
| Say or Do? | They said they would | They showed intent | They actually did it |
| Investment? | Free/easy action | Some effort/commitment | Real money or reputation |
| How many? | 1-5 people | 6-20 people | 21+ people |

**Evidence Score = Total / 9 × 100%**

Example:
- 5 people said they'd pay (Say=1, Investment=1, Sample=1) → 33% weak
- 15 people pre-ordered (Do=3, Investment=3, Sample=2) → 89% strong

## Confidence Meter

Each project has one number: **Confidence %**

Calculated from:
- Number of experiments run
- Strength of evidence collected
- Balance of supporting vs refuting evidence

```
Confidence: ████████████░░░░░░░░ 58%

Supporting evidence: 5 pieces (avg 72% strength)
Refuting evidence: 2 pieces (avg 45% strength)
Experiments run: 4
```

**What it means:**
- 0-30%: Not confident. Need more/stronger evidence.
- 30-60%: Getting there. Key hypotheses still untested.
- 60-80%: Confident. Ready for bigger investment.
- 80-100%: Very confident. Strong evidence across all areas.

## Insights

### Types

| Type | What It Is | Points |
|------|------------|--------|
| **Observation** | Basic data point. "We saw X." | 5 |
| **Insight** | Useful learning. "X means Y." | 20 |
| **Key Insight** | Changes our approach. | 50 |
| **Killer Insight** | Major breakthrough. Rare. | 500 |

### Killer Insight Criteria

A Killer Insight:
- Has strong evidence (≥80% score)
- Changes everything (pivot, kill, or major unlock)
- Reviewed and confirmed by 2+ team members
- Goes into the Insights Vault permanently
