## One-Liner

OS Research is a startup validation studio that de-risks early-stage investing by running structured experiments on startup ideas before anyone writes a check — then co-investing alongside partners in the winners.

---

## The Problem

Vietnam's startup ecosystem is not failing because of a lack of money or ambition. It is failing because of a lack of quality.

1. **The quality of entrepreneurship is low.** There is almost no real education on how to build innovative companies in Vietnam. Universities teach business administration, not company building. Founders don't know how to identify real problems, design solutions, or test ideas with rigor. The result: a generation of would-be entrepreneurs who copy what's trending instead of solving what matters.

2. **The ecosystem chases hype, not value.** Too much capital flows into quick money grabs — crypto plays, dropshipping clones, and trend-riding copycats with no defensibility and no intention of building something durable. The culture rewards fast exits and vanity metrics over real businesses that serve real needs. The founders who could build category-defining companies are drowned out by noise.

3. **Deal flow is poor because the source is poor.** Investors in Vietnam don't have a deal flow problem — they have a deal quality problem. When the pool of founders is untrained, undisciplined, and chasing hype, no amount of sourcing fixes the pipeline. The problem is upstream: nobody is building the entrepreneurs, the ideas, or the evidence that would make early-stage investing rational.

**The gap is not capital. The gap is a system that produces validated, investable ventures.**

---

## The Solution

OS Research eliminates the guesswork from early-stage investing.

**We don't wait for deal flow. We create it.**

Everything starts from within. Our ideas come from our own vision, our own research, and our own thesis about what Vietnam needs. We study the market, identify blue-ocean opportunities, and generate venture concepts internally.

Then we test them. Every idea goes through a structured validation process — real experiments, real users, real data. We know for sure if an idea is good or bad. There is no guessing.

The good ones get funded. The bad ones get killed. Simple.

We are not an accelerator waiting for applications. We are not consultants giving advice. We are a studio that originates ideas, proves them, builds them, and invests in them.

---

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Originate**
Ideas are generated internally from OSR's thesis-driven research across five target sectors. We study markets, identify structural gaps, and design venture concepts that solve real problems. We don't wait for founders to come to us — we build the pipeline from within.

**Step 2 — Test**
Every idea enters as a project. First, a discovery phase — quick, cheap signal testing (social listening, demand-test ads) to kill bad ideas early. Budget: up to 50M VND. Ideas that survive discovery move into full experimentation — fake-door MVPs, customer interviews, prototype testing, purchase signal validation. Total experiment budget: up to $10K per project. Timeline: 2-8 weeks.

**Step 3 — Decide**
The data tells us yes or no. If the evidence says there's real demand, real willingness to pay, and a viable path to profit — we proceed. If not, we kill it. Fast and cheap. No ego. No sunk cost fallacy.

**Step 4 — Invest**
Validated projects receive seed funding of up to 3B VND (~$120K). OSR co-invests alongside angel partners. Founders are selected and brought in at this stage — carefully chosen operators who share our vision and are committed to building for the long term. Founders pay $0 — we take equity, not fees.

**Step 5 — Operate**
OSR doesn't walk away after the check. We stay as operator and builder — running growth experiments, hiring, building product, and scaling the company alongside the founder.
